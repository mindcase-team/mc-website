Mindcase delivers end-to-end data solutions—combining powerful AI technology with hands-on service. We handle everything from large-scale data collection (web, APIs, files) to cleaning, structuring, analysis, and intelligence delivery. Whether it's powering due diligence, competitive research, or automated reporting, we build custom workflows that turn complex data into clear, actionable outcomes—fast.

What Are the Use Cases Where I Can Partner with Mindcase?

We work with partners across investment research, consulting, market intelligence, operations, sales enablement, and more. If your work relies on timely, structured, multi-source data—or if your clients need faster, smarter insights—we can collaborate to deliver them.

How Long Does It Take to Set Up Mindcase for a Client or Project?

Most engagements are up and running in a few days. Since we deliver both the tech and the team, setup is fast, flexible, and requires minimal lift from your side. We scope, configure, and deploy tailored solutions to fit your workflow and timelines.

Why Should I Partner with Mindcase?

Partnering with mindcase means faster, smarter outcomes for your clients—with less effort on your side. We bring the tech, the expertise, and the execution—so you can focus on strategy, not wrangling data. Our solutions save time, reduce costs, and scale cleanly across teams and use cases.

Does Mindcase Offer White-Label or Embedded Delivery Options?

Yes. We offer white-label delivery, embedded outputs (e.g., data feeds, dashboards), and branded workflows for partners who want to bring mindcase’s capabilities into their own client experience.
